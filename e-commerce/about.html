ABOUT US

My mission is to create clothing that make women feel beautiful, bold and empowered by offering styles, silhouettes and colors that will make women centre of attention.
I design Kaela Kay for women who love the artistic representation of themselves in the way they dress – and it's my charge to help all women identify their personal style in their fabulous size.
For this reason Kaela Kay stocks up to size US22 and will custom design for any fabulous size.

Often recognized for brand identity and for cultivating a strong brand aesthetic, I strives to make Kaela Kay a global brand for transformative fashion.
